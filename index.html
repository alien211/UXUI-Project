NEWS

The inspection is carried out on April 19 - if you have not made a specific appointment with the caretaker. We recommend that one resident is present during the inspection.


  • Floors and staircases must be washed thoroughly.
  • Stove and ovens must be cleaned from the inside and the outside as well.
  • All glass parts on windows and doors must be cleaned.

    • See the full cleaning plan on Ravnsbjerg Kollegiet's website.

    Distance and arrangement

    Always keep at least 1 meter away from each other. You should keep a distance to others, when you stay together in all forms of common space. If necessary, relocate the furniture.


    Extra cleaning

    Remember to clean the contact points, such as handles, handrails, switches, tableedges, utensils, taps, toilets, etc..


    Recommendations (common areas)

  • Make distance markings
  • Place hand sanitiser at all entrances
  • Remind each other to use the elbow instead of the fingers - when possible
  • Use signs with number of people allowed
  • Air out 2 x 10 minutes every day

    • Read more recommendations from the Danish Health Authority, as well as how to behave in case of infection on Ravnsbjerg Kollegiet's website.

    FACILITIES

    MULTIMEDIA ROOM

    Our multimedia room is perfect for students who would like to work together on a school project or just want to enjoy a nice movie. The room has a projector and many tables and chairs so it's ready for any group to be rented out. Contact the residents' council to borrow a key to the multimedia room. Check out the dorm's website to read the rules of the multimedia room before renting it out.

    GYM

    Our gym is located in the basement of building 40. There you can find several gym equipment such as a treadmill or a bench and many weights that inhabitants can use to exercise. Read further information on the dorm's website such as rules of the gym and monthly fee.

    MUSIC ROOM

    The music room is equipped with several instruments such as a piano or drums. There's also 2 stereo speakers for students to use. Ask the caretaker for the keys if you would like to use the room.
