FACILITIES

MULTIMEDIA ROOM

Our multimedia room is perfect for students who would like to work together on a school project or just want to enjoy a nice movie. The room has a projector and many tables and chairs so it's ready for any group to be rented out. Contact the residents' council to borrow a key to the multimedia room. Check out the dorm's website to read the rules of the multimedia room before renting it out.

GYM

Our gym is located in the basement of building 40. There you can find several gym equipment such as a treadmill or a bench and many weights that inhabitants can use to exercise. Read further information on the dorm's website such as rules of the gym and monthly fee.

MUSIC ROOM

The music room is equipped with several instruments such as a piano or drums. There's also 2 stereo speakers for students to use. Ask the caretaker for the keys if you would like to use the room.
